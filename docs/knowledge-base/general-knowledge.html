S2DR3 and S2DR4 (S2DRx) General Knowledge

Temporal availability

S2DRx can super-resolve and cloud-mitigate Sentinel-2 scenes from January 1st, 2018 onward with the same cadence (5-day revisit). The full scene catalog is available on Copernicus Hub website.

Min and Max Area Of Interes (AOI)

AOI definition is based on a bounding box or a polygon in GeoJSON.
The minimum technical AOI size is 0.4 km2 (40 ha), as quality of results over smaller areas would be compromised.
The maximum technical AOI size is 150 km2 (undergoing improvement).

Output Products of S2DRx

S2DRx output will contain 4 products:

  • S2L2Ax10_T[MGRS]-[UID]-[DATE]_MS.tif – 10-band multi-spectal image
  • S2L2Ax10_T[MGRS]-[UID]-[DATE]_TCI.tif – True colour RGB image
  • S2L2Ax10_T[MGRS]-[UID]-[DATE]_NDVI.tif – Pseudo-colour NDVI image
  • S2L2Ax10_T[MGRS]-[UID]-[DATE]_IRP.tif – Ifra-red preudo-colour image
