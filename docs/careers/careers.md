# Careers at Gamma Earth

We are building the next generation of AI-powered geospatial solutions and are looking for talented people to join us.

---

## Full-Stack Software Engineer (Remote or Hybrid – based in Switzerland)

**Location:** Remote or hybrid — candidate must be **based in Switzerland**  
**Employment:** Full-time  
**Start:** As soon as possible

### About Gamma Earth

Gamma Earth develops cutting-edge AI and super-resolution technology for Earth observation. Our products—including **S2DR3** (Sentinel-2 10-band 10× super-resolution API) and **S2DR4** (super-resolution + cloud mitigation API)—are used by organizations in agriculture, climate, environment, and satellite sensor design. We are now expanding into a **SaaS webGIS platform** and scaling our cloud-based services globally.

We are a small, fast-moving, impact-driven team. If you want to shape the future of geospatial analytics and AI-powered Earth observation, this is the right place.

### Your Role

We’re looking for a **Full-Stack Software Engineer** to help build and scale our cloud-native geospatial and AI services. You will work across the stack—from backend APIs to frontend components—and collaborate directly with our founders and engineering team.

You will play a key role in developing our SaaS platform, integrating AI workflows, improving deployment pipelines, and supporting customer-facing applications.

### What You Will Work On

- Build and maintain cloud-native services on **Google Cloud** and/or **AWS**
- Develop and extend **REST APIs**, microservices, and backend pipelines
- Work with geospatial data formats (GeoTIFF, vector data, STAC, WMS/WFS)
- Integrate with our AI super-resolution engines (Python, GPU workflows)
- Build user-facing web applications (e-commerce, dashboards, WebGIS)
- Implement automated deployment, CI/CD, and monitoring
- Contribute to architectural decisions as we scale our SaaS products

### Must-Have Qualifications

- 3+ years of professional experience in **full-stack software engineering**
- Strong experience with **cloud platforms** (Google Cloud, AWS, or both)
- Solid skills in **Python, JavaScript/TypeScript**, and modern web frameworks
- Experience with **Docker**, containers, CI/CD pipelines
- Familiarity with databases (PostgreSQL; PostGIS is a plus)
- Comfortable working in a fast-paced, product-driven environment

### Strong Plus (Not Required, but Highly Valued)

- Experience with **geospatial stacks** (GDAL, Rasterio, PostGIS, GeoPandas, xarray, STAC)
- Experience building or integrating **webGIS** platforms (Leaflet, Mapbox GL, OpenLayers)
- Knowledge of **machine learning**, PyTorch or TensorFlow
- Experience deploying GPU workloads in the cloud
- Background in Earth observation, agriculture, climate, or satellite imaging

### Soft Skills We Value

- Independent, proactive problem solver
- Comfortable in an early-stage, high-ownership environment
- Strong communication and collaboration skills
- Curiosity about AI, Earth observation, or sustainability

### What We Offer

- Remote or hybrid work (**based in Switzerland**)
- Competitive compensation
- Work on real, impactful geospatial + AI products used globally
- High ownership, direct influence on product and architecture decisions
- Opportunity to grow with the company as we scale
