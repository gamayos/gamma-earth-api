5.2 Credits and Processing Area

Credits are the unit used to purchase and account for processing capacity. Credits translate to a processing area in km² at a conversion rate that varies by geographic region, as published in the Fee Policy. The applicable rate is determined by the location of the AOI submitted for processing.

5.3 Professional Plan — Features and Downgrade

The Professional Plan is activated automatically upon any Top Up purchase. It is valid for one calendar month from the date of the Top Up purchase that activated or most recently renewed it, and is automatically renewed upon each subsequent Top Up purchase made before expiry. If no further Top Up is purchased before expiry, the Account reverts to the Base Plan.

Reversion does not affect purchased Credits remaining in the Account, which remain valid and consumable for twenty-four (24) months from their purchase date, but access to Professional Plan features is suspended until the next Top Up. Upon activating the Professional Plan, users gain access to:

  • Access to S2DR3 and S2DR4 via Cloud API and SDK;
  • Batch processing of large areas and time series;
  • Arbitrary bounding boxes or GeoJSON polygon AOI definitions;
  • Processing of complete Sentinel-2 MGRS tiles;
  • Processing on an H3 hexagonal grid;
  • Integration with local storage and Google Drive.
